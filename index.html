Video Editing

Video editing is the process of cutting, combining, and enhancing raw video footage with effects, transitions, text, and audio. It is used to make storytelling more engaging or to deliver a message more clearly. Video editors perform tasks such as trimming clips, adding transitions, inserting background music, and creating subtitles. Popular video editing software includes Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, and CapCut.

Graphic Designing

Graphic design is the art and practice of creating visual content to communicate messages. Designers use typography, imagery, color, and layout techniques to craft visuals for both print and digital media. Whether it’s a logo, poster, website interface, or social media post, graphic design helps capture attention and convey information effectively. Tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Canva are commonly used in the industry. In today’s digital age, graphic design plays a crucial role in branding, marketing, and user experience.
